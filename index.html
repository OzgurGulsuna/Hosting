About me

I am an innovator, maker and a self-taught artist who has an inexhaustible passion for visual effects, film tech, robotics, art(any kind of it), coding, physics, and astronomy. I like to work collaboratively across disciplines as you might guess. I also like learning new tools, workflows, and techniques. I have always enjoyed using my imagination to envision new ideas. I am currently studying mechanical engineering at middle east technical university. But I would like to enlarge my vision by learning more technical details about electronics, computer science, and biotechnology which is just one of my goals. If you have an exciting project that you are interested in working together on, get in touch!
